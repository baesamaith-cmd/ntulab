Lab Overview

Research that connects theory, design, and manufacturing

The Design Sciences Laboratory at NTU focuses on applying sciences, economic theory, and AI to the design of customized and sustainable products, services, and systems. Our work includes multidisciplinary design optimization, advanced modeling, and digital twin technology for the smart factory of the future.

What defines our work

  • Integration of AI, economic theory, and design science
  • Focus on sustainable and customized product lifecycle
  • Pioneering smart manufacturing and digital twin solutions
