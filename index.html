Lab Overview

Research that connects theory, systems, and societal use

The Advanced Computing Lab at NTU studies intelligent computing systems that must be both technically strong and practically deployable. Our work spans machine learning, systems engineering, and human-computer interaction, with an emphasis on reliability, scalability, and measurable real-world value.

What defines our work

  • Method development informed by real deployment constraints
  • Interdisciplinary collaboration across AI, systems, design, and domain experts
  • Commitment to reproducibility, openness, and public-interest impact
