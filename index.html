Lab Overview

Research that bridges theory and deployment

The Advanced Computing Lab at NTU investigates intelligent computing systems that can scale responsibly. We combine algorithmic research, systems engineering, and user-centered design to solve consequential problems in health, sustainability, and digital infrastructure.

What defines our work

  • Strong technical depth with practical deployment goals
  • Interdisciplinary collaboration across AI, systems, and design
  • Commitment to reproducibility, openness, and social impact
